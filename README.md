The Idea:
Create a mobile game, that is golf! Tap and pull back to aim and give strength, then when you let go, a mini game of stop
the cursor mini game to see how accurate you are with you shot. Each course of golf is going to be a rouge like, with an over all
leveling progress of either better balls, or better clubs. Ideas for better clubs would be more leeway on the cursor mini game, or
when you pull back with that club, you can see more of where it is going to go (think dots that show about where the ball will go,
worse clubs, less dots so less info on how far they will go or even maybe width? better clubs, shows the dots further and the width goes
more narrow, to give you a better idea.)

18 holes a course, with the option of playing the front or back 9 only, to keep games shorter. Art style will be similar to the paper apps
golf notebook (https://gladdendesign.com/products/paper-apps-golf) so very minimalist.
